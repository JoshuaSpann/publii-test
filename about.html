About

Publii is a static-website CMS. It's like Wordpress, but doesn't use a whole LAMP stack to implement. It is a tool to quickly build, manage, and deploy websites like a CMS without the need of a dynamic, PHP and MySQL driven environment.

Publii can be integrated into GitHub or GitLab pages as a method for you to display your website! Publii is simple and familiar for Wordpress users, though the layout differences will take a while to get used to... I still like to do raw HTML, CSS, JS and Markdown.....
